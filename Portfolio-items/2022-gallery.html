Description

In 2022, our seniors managed to regain Senior status in the league, narrowly missing out in the league final to Curry. Led by Willie Gormley and a team of club stalwarts, the club fought well to a intermediate championship semi-final, losing out to St. Molaise Gaels, who went on to win the final convincingly. Our u21s, amalgamated with Ballymote GAA, won the "A" county final, beating St Molaise Gaels in the final.

Published: January 5th, 2023
