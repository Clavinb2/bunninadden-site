Match Summary

Bunninadden reached the 2006 Sligo Senior Football Championship Final, facing local rivals Curry at Markievicz Park. Despite leading 2-4 to 0-4 at half-time thanks to two goals from S. McHugh, Curry fought back strongly in the second half. A. Marren's 8 points proved decisive as Curry outscored Bunninadden by 11 points to 5 in the second period to claim a 3-point victory.

Competition: Sligo SFC Final

Date: 24 September 2006

Venue: Markievicz Park

Result: Curry 1-15, Bunninadden 2-9

Manager: Brian Touhy
