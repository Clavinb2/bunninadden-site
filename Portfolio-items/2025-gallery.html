Football Report 2025

The club welcomed a new management team for 2025 as Shane Spellman and Cillian Doyle became the first outside coaches to take the intermediate panel in a long number of years. They were aided by our own Joe Scanlon and later in the championship by Michael Gormley. Jonathan Hodgins performed the role as Captain. We thank them all for their service to the club.

Division 2 League & Spring League

The Spring league was altered to reflect the teams competing in the Intermediate championship. We recorded wins against Geevagh, St Pats and Calry/St Joseph's to book a place in the final against Ballymote. It was agreed by both clubs that the Division 2 League fixture between both teams would double up as the Spring League final.

This year we competed once again in Division 2 of the League, with results detailed below.

2025 Season Summary

The 2025 season saw Bunninadden compete in Division 2 of the League and the Intermediate Football Championship. With a new management team of Shane Spellman and Cillian Doyle, assisted by Joe Scanlon and Michael Gormley, and captained by Jonathan Hodgins, the team showed great promise throughout the year.
