Celebrating 25 Years: 2000-2025

In 2000, Bunninadden won the Senior Championship and Owen B Hunt Cup for the second time in 109 years, beating Coolera Strandhill with a last minute Padraig Doohan goal. This collection of memorabilia celebrates that historic achievement, featuring match programmes, scrapbooks, photographs, and personal mementos from players and supporters.

About This Collection

This commemorative collection celebrates the 25th anniversary of Bunninadden's historic 2000 Senior Championship victory. Under the guidance of James Kerins, Richard Brennan and John Spellman, the club secured its first senior title in living memory - only the second in 109 years since 1891.

The winning goal came from Padraig Doohan in the dying moments against Coolera Strandhill, sparking scenes of jubilation throughout south Sligo.
