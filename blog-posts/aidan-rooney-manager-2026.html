Aidan Rooney Ratified as Senior Team Manager for 2026

  1. Home
  2. Recent News
  3. Aidan Rooney Manager 2026

Aidan Rooney Managing St Mary's

Barry Clavin December 22, 2025 Club News

Bunninadden GAA is delighted to announce the appointment and ratification of Aidan Rooney as Manager of our Senior Football Team for the 2026 season.

Aidan brings a lifetime of experience in the game, both as an elite inter-county player and as a proven coach and manager across club and county structures. His track record in player development and his understanding of what it takes to build successful teams make him an exciting addition as we look ahead to the year to come.

A Decorated Playing Career

Aidan enjoyed a distinguished playing career with Glencar Manorhamilton (1984–2003), where he played a key role in a standout era for the club, including:

  • Senior League Champions (1998)
  • Senior Championship Champions (1999)
  • Player-Manager (2002)

He also lined out at senior club level with St. Mary's (2004).

At inter-county level, Aidan was a committed servant to Leitrim Senior Football from 1987 to 2001, gaining experience at the highest level of the game over a 15-year period.

Aidan Rooney playing for Leitrim

Proven Leadership in Management and Coaching

Aidan's managerial and coaching résumé is equally impressive, highlighted by a long-standing commitment to underage coaching and multiple senior management roles. His experience includes:

  • St. Mary's Underage Coach (2004–2025)
  • Sligo Minor Manager (2014, 2015 & 2025)
  • Glencar Manorhamilton Senior Manager (2016 & 2017)
  • St. Farnan's Intermediate Manager (2018)
  • St. Mary's Senior Manager (2019, 2020 & 2021)

This breadth of involvement — particularly his sustained work in underage development — aligns strongly with the values of Bunninadden GAA and our focus on building from within.

Aidan Rooney as selector for Sligo GAA

Looking Ahead

Everyone in the club would like to welcome Aidan to Bunninadden and wish him the very best as he begins preparations for the 2026 campaign. Further details on the senior management team and pre-season plans will be shared in due course.

Fáilte, Aidan — and best of luck for 2026.
