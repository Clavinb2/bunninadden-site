Club Membership 2023

Our club membership for 2023 will be continued online. Please have a glance over them and if you have any questions contact Club Secretary & Registrar Keith Henry on 086 335 7182 or email the Club Secretary

Who does it affect?

All members be they players or non-players should register on this system. If you know of someone unable to access the internet, please let us know and we contact them.

Is the membership rate changing?

No. Full Membership and Family membership remains at the same rate as previous years. However adult Students in Full Time education have a reduced rate this year. There is also a Social Membership at a reduced rate for people who do not want Full Membership.

Will there be a club members draw this year?

Yes.

How do you pay membership?

You can pay online with your bank card in a secure and safe manner. You have the option to pay in one full instalment or three equal ones. You still can pay cash or cheque to the club, but you need to register on the system and select that option.

Is there a deadline?

All players both underage and adult must be paid before they partake in any training sessions or games.

Currently under the rules of the GAA, membership should be paid no later than March 31st to cover insurance for players to play and train and to give full membership privileges for all Full Members.

How do I sign up?

** If you have signed up already in 2021 it is just a case of renewing your membership for 2022 by logging in and renewing here gaa.ie **

For first time registration follow this guide below.

1. Go to gaa.ieand click on the register link if you have never used the system for COVID19 health questionnaires last year.

2. Enter the relevant information into the required fields.

Usernames must be unique, therefore please choose another username to create your account if you receive an ‘Invalid Username’ error message.

Verify your Email address

Check your inbox and click on the link within the email.

  • check spam or junk mail folders if the email does not appear within your inbox.

    • Complete your profile

    Enter the additional information into the mandatory fields. To add your club, you will need to select GAA, Sligo and Bunninadden from the dropdown options.

  • You can have multiple clubs listed in your profile (GAA & LGFA so St Nathy’s if you have anyone playing with them). Add the Ballymote/Bunninadden amalgamation too if you have a coaching role or children playing in it.

    • Select your membership option

    Select whichever membership option applies to you. If you pick family, please name the children you wish to register.

    Select payment option

  • You can pay online using your bank or one full payment or three instalments
  • You can still pay directly to the club by cash or cheque. If you wish to do this option, click the option to “Pay in Club“.
